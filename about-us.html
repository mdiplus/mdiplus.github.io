About MDI:

Maximus Development Inc is a builder with a passion for results. Based in the Sacramento, California, Maximus Development Inc., independent provider of Construction solutions to the homeowners, banks, investors, management companies and insurance industry. We are comprised of a qualified team of professionals who provide all construction services. The firm's executive background provides the expertise for a one-stop project strategy without compromising quality. Our ability to provide all phases of a project from start to finish, no matter how extensive, allows us to provide pricing below industry cost by eliminating high overhead and contract pricing. We listen to your goals. We develop strategies based on your business. We track our performance. We do everything we can to earn your trust and build great lasting relationships.


After many years of experience in the industry, the three owners of Maximus Development Inc., developed the vision of a company built on one of the principles that made America the leading nation in business innovation and development: "Work as a Team." Built fundamentally around customer service, MDI ensures that every project is executed with the goal of creating customer satisfaction and building a lasting relationship.
