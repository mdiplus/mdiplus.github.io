REO Services from MDI:

Maximus Development Inc. offer many REO rehabilitation services to real estate professionals, financial institutions, outsourcers, and asset managers. We have outstanding field estimators throught California, who can give you and accurate estimate of the work that needs to be done, along with a list of all items needing repair and their cost. This gives our client all the necessary information to make an informed and educated decision about which repairs should be completed.

MDI is comprised of a qualified team of professionals who provide all construction services. We provide a wide range of services from a full rebuild or remodel to demolition, as well as the maintenance and preservation of your property after the work has been completed. Our ability to provide all phases of a project from start to finish, no matter how extensive, allows us to provide pricing below industry cost by eliminating high overhead and contract pricing.
