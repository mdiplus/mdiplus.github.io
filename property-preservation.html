Property Preservation:

Property preservation is of utmost importance to retaining the value of your assets. With MDI, our highly trained team moves in quickly to ensure that each of your properties is safe, secure and ready to be shown to potential buyers. We follow your guidelines and all state and local regulations to create a custom package of services that best suits your needs.

Our comprehensive, start-to-finish property preservation service begins with initial rekeys and securing, then we move on to debris removal, lawn care, winterization and dewinterization, cleaning, plumbing, periodic maintenance and more. In the end, we effectively prepare properties for sale. There is no other property preservation company who does more.
