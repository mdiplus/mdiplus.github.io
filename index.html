Our mission:

When you contract with a builder, you want to ensure that there is a balance between budget, time and money.

With respect to budget, every project we do is carried out under written contract. It sets out the details of the project including dollars, terms, time schedule and specifications. Our attention to detail before construction starts, together with our clients, eliminates the need for cost over-runs unless the client decides to make a change.


We take whatever time is required to sort through the details of the work with our clients before finalizing the contract. This is a very important part of the process. The planning time will be well spent as it will ensure a smoother construction process, little or no stress along the way and completion within the contract time.


We take pride in every project we undertake and are committed to a top quality finished project. We choose our products and techniques very carefully. We are also careful to engage sub-traders who we are confident will do a superb job. We realize it is the little details that make all the difference, and we want our clients to be completely satisfied with their home and take pride in its craftsmanship.


Building or renovating a home may be one of the biggest and exciting projects you will undertake in your life. We are committed to working closely with our clients to make sure the entire process is smooth and enjoyable.
